Info about Carlsbad, CA

Carlsbad has things to do for the whole family. Legoland Resort, which delights all ages, makes its home in Carlsbad. If you're a golfer be sure and check out our award-winning golf-courses: Champions Golf Course, Legends Golf Course, Aviara Golf Club and The Crossings at Carlsbad. We also have wonderful beaches like the Tamarack Beach, Carlsbad City Beach and Terramar Beach. While you are doing any or all of these activities don't forget to hydrate and what better way to hydrate than with a healthy, delicious beverage from Bountiful Foods? It's like nature in a cup.
