About Us

Our History

As a mom that was pressed for time, Jennie Crowther wanted to ensure that she and her children still received the proper nutrition. Making wholesome meals takes a while, so Jennie started turning fresh, local fruits into delicious smoothies that she and her kids could drink when they were on their way to soccer tournaments. Her kids would ask for drinks for their friends who soon were asking for drinks for their friends! Soon, she had a food truck set up outside the soccer matches to service the soccer players and their parents. Bountiful Foods was born. Jennie uses only the freshest, local ingredients because she wants to give all of her customers the most nutrition.

History of Carlsbad

Carlsbad started as a whistle stop on the new California Southern Railroad. In the 1880s John Frazier, another former ship's captain, dug a fresh-water well that produced mineral water. The water was analyzed and found equal in mineral content to one of Europe's most popular health spas--Karlsbad, Bohemia. We use this same water in every beverage that we sell.
More information on the area history, including a tour of historic points, can be found at carlsbad.ca.us.
