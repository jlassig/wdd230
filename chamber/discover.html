Discover Syracuse

Welcome to Syracuse, "The Gateway To Antelope Island". Syracuse is located in Davis County, Utah. It is sandwiched between Salt Lake City, which is 30 miles south, and Ogden, which is 15 miles north. Also, the Great Salt Lake is to the west and I-15 is to the east. The city has been rapidly growing since the 1990s. Once the West Davis Corridor part of Legacy Highway gets completed in 2024, there will be even more growth in Syracuse since it will have easier freeway access to Salt Lake City.

Syracuse is the gateway to Antelope Island Statepark.

Bluff Trail is popular for bikers, runners and walkers.

Chloe Sunshine Park was designed for accessibility and serves children of all abilities.

Syracuse Temple for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. It is currently scheduled to be completed July 2024.

The Syracuse Museum tells the history of the early development of Syracuse.

Jensen Nature Park has a 2.6 mile loop trail that is accessible for wheelchairs and strollers, a pond, a water-feature and is a popular place for families to play, enjoy nature and take photographs.
